About ShortcutIV

Welcome Trainers! I originally registered this domain when the only PvP IV checking website went down for an extended period of time, and decided I could build my own instead. It was a fun challenge, and now has blossomed into a playground for any of my Pokemon Go related tool ideas, such as our Type Lookup chart generator and companion Typing Quiz (now with actual in-game multiplier values!)

Please feel free to reach out on Discord if you have any ideas of how to improve this site, or have ideas of what should be built next. Happy hunting!
